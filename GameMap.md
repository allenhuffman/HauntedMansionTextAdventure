## Room Connections Summary

### Main Path Through Mansion:
1. **Foyer** (1) → **Portrait Gallery** (2) → **Hallway** (3,4) → **Marble Busts** (5)
2. **Bottom of Stairs** (6) → **Piano Room** (7) → **Library** (8,9) → **Portrait Hallway** (10)
3. **Bottom of Stairs** (11) → **Hallway** (12) → **Servant's Quarters** (13)

### Grand Ballroom Complex:
- **Piano Room** (7) → **Hallway** (14) → **Grand Ballroom** (15,16,17,20)
- **Kitchen** (18,19) connects to ballroom
- **Outside Patio** (21) accessible from ballroom

### Second Floor:
- **Conservatory** (23) → **Hallway of Doors** (24,25) → **Clock** (26) → **Seance Room** (27)
- **Balcony** (29,30,31,32) overlooks ballroom
- **Walkway** (34,35,36) also overlooks ballroom
- **Endless Hallway** complex (37-45) with secret rooms

### Attic Level:
- **Attic rooms** (47,48,49,50,51) with **Ghostly Bride** (51)
- **Ledge** (52) overlooking graveyard

## Vertical Connections (UP/DOWN):
- **[6] ↕ [22]**: Main stairs (Bottom ↔ Top)
- **[11] ↕ [28]**: Secondary stairs (Bottom ↔ Hallway)  
- **[17] ↕ [34]**: Ballroom to Walkway
- **[33] ↕ [46]**: Stairs to Attic

## Key Locations:
- **Starting Point**: Foyer (1)
- **Major Gathering**: Grand Ballroom (15,16,17,20)
- **Mysterious Areas**: Seance Room (27), Secret Rooms (43,45)
- **Final Challenge**: Attic with Ghostly Bride (51)
- **Dead End**: Ledge overlooking graveyard (52)
